Kitabuk | Digital Diary

This prototype digital diary exists as part of a research project aimed at understanding a high-level set of guidelines to develop an engaging online digital diary system to meet usersâ€™ expectations.

Caregiving can be a heavy burden for many people and the emotional side of caregiving can surface soon after an individual takes on caregiving duties. creating a web-based diary that enables caregivers to self-report their emotions, reducing their burden and alleviating their stress, is crucial for a digitised future and a caregiver's future

My hope is that I can develop Kitabuk | Digital Diary further than this research project, so that it can be recognised as the go-to digital diary within the caregiver community. It is a project that I am very proud of and I am excited to share it with you all.
